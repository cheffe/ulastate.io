Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 10, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Ulastate ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in relation to our fruit value calculator web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

Ulastate is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The application operates entirely in your web browser.

Local Storage

The only data stored by our application is your language preference, which is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Is used solely to remember your language selection for future visits

Cookies

We do not use cookies on this website.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. There are no external scripts or services that collect data about your visit.

Server Logs

When you visit our website, standard web server logs may be created by the hosting provider. These logs typically contain:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited

These logs are maintained by the hosting provider for security and technical purposes and are not directly accessed or analyzed by us for tracking purposes.

Your Calculations

All calculations you perform using the fruit value calculator happen entirely in your browser. No calculation data is sent to our servers or stored anywhere beyond your current browser session.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

This project is open source and available under the MIT License. You can review the complete source code on GitHub.

Contact
